    A taste of tradition, a sip of innovation.

    Our Story

    Founded in 2015 by Chef Gusteau, Sandwich&Co was born from a passion for bringing authentic flavors to our community. We are committed to using the finest, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, and creating a warm, inviting space where everyone feels at home. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a beloved local destination, known for our Signature Club Sandwich and unwavering dedication to quality.

    Our Philosophy

    At Sandwich&Co, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's an experience. We strive to create moments of connection and joy with every meal, celebrating the simple pleasures of life and the bonds of community. We are dedicated to sustainability, fostering a welcoming environment, and making a positive impact on the world around us.
